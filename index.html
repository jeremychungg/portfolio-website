Introduction

I am an Electrical Engineering student at the University of Pennsylvania pursuing a BSE with a concentration in robotics. My work focuses on embedded systems, autonomous robotics, and building hardware-software systems that interact with the physical world.

I enjoy designing and prototyping real systems — from sensor interfaces and control electronics to embedded firmware and robotics platforms. My experience spans research, engineering teams, and hands-on projects, including work with Penn Aerial Robotics, Science Olympiad at Penn, and robotics research collaborations in both the United States and Germany.

I am particularly interested in robotics, aerospace systems, and intelligent machines that operate in complex environments. Long term, I hope to work on technologies that expand human capability in domains like autonomous systems, space infrastructure, and advanced robotics.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy building personal hardware projects, learning new fabrication tools, photography, and exploring emerging technologies at the intersection of robotics, AI, and space.
